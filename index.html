ABC Glossary Questionnaire

language is a powerful tool of e-colonialism (electronic colonialism). this questionnaire is a resource created to help you reflect on the interconnected relationship between technology, nature, and your community that is embedded in language. this questionnaire is part of a growing glossary for an anti-colonial Black feminist critical media ecology — abc glossary for short — calling for a more abundant relationship between our technological infrastructure, nature, and ourselves to imagine new terms & conditions.

if you would like for your submission to be attributed to you, please provide your name above. please note that submissions may be included in the abc glossary and may be featured in my newsletter, New Terms & Conditions. subscribe to my newsletter to be updated when abc glossary is live. ask a question or get in touch.
